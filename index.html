Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Smart Repair Tracker system work?
When your vehicle enters our garage in Athurugiriya, we record your contact details and assign a unique Job ID (e.g. TDC-303). Every time our technicians complete a stage (Diagnostics, Parts approval, Active repairing, QC/Calibrate, Handover), our database automatically sends you an SMS and Email notification containing detailed updates, so you don't have to call and check.
Do I need to book in advance, or can I walk in?
We highly recommend booking via our online appointment scheduler or by calling our hotline. Because we specialize in advanced mechanical troubleshooting and suspension calibrations, scheduling in advance ensures a dedicated lift bay and diagnostic engineer are immediately available when you arrive. Emergency accident towing vehicles bypass this slot requirement.
What type of vehicles do you specialize in?
We specialize in modern passenger cars, SUVs, and luxury sedans. We have dedicated software diagnostic rigs for European, Japanese, and Korean brands. Our team holds specific diagnostic calibrations for advanced electrical networks, dual-clutch transmissions, and active hydraulic suspension valves.
Do you coordinate directly with insurance companies?
Yes. For accident recovery, we produce detailed structural assessments, photo documentation, and electronic diagnostic codes to coordinate with all major insurance firms in Sri Lanka, expediting your claim approval.
Where is your workshop located in Athurugiriya?
Our workshop is Total Drive Care Solutions (PVT) LTD located in Athurugiriya. We have an embedded interactive Google Map at the bottom of the page showing our precise location, complete with driving directions.
